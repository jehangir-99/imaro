HOW TO BUY
BACKPACK WALLET
Download Backpack or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play store for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to backpack.app
GET SOME SOLANA
Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $IMARO. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Backpack, transfer from another wallet, bridge from any chain, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
GO TO JUPITER
Connect to Jupiter or your favourite AMM on Solana. Go to jup.ag in your web browser or on the browser inside your Backpack app. Connect your wallet. Choose SOL in the "You're paying" section. Paste the $IMARO token address into the "To receive" section. Confirm via your wallet and when it prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
SWAP SOL FOR $IMARO
Swap SOL for $IMARO. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
